Wide range of car models

Our fleet includes over 100 latest generation car models, of different classes, including Premium brands, manual or automatic transmission cars, 4x4 SUV, Diesel or Hybrid engine cars.

Reserved car model warranty

We strive to offer you the car you want. Over 90% of our customers received the exact reserved car model.

Smart rates & no hidden fees

For each available car, you will receive 3 offers (Budget, Standard, Plus) , with clear information about rates, insurances, deposit/responsibility, included benefits and optional services.

24/7 services & assistance

You can pick-up and return your rented car at any time of day and night. In addition, the +40752220222 emergency phone number is non-stop available.

One-way and international rental

For flexible travels, we offer one-way rentals. On special terms and conditions, you can travel abroad with selected car models.

Optional services and additional protection

For a more comfortable and safer trip, you can choose for optional services (child seat, GPS, snow chains, additional driver etc.) and for additional protection plans and reduction of the user`s responsibility.

Smart rates
